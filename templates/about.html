This simple app automates common tasks when you will be out of office:

  • Create public event in your calendar - helps other people scheduling meetings when they know that you're out of office
  • If you have team/department shared calendars then this app will search for them and put your name in that calendar
  • Reject all meetings with message why you're out of office
  • If you're the organizer of the event you will grant edit access to guests, so that they can postpone event or cancel conference room when it's not needed
  • Certain meetings when there might be only one attendee attending (e.g. One on One's) will be cancelled

If you want to improve this app, visit GitHub page.
